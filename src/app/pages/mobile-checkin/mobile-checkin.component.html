NOVOAIR Mobile Check In

NOVOAIR mobile app lets you Check-in online from anywhere and save time at the airport. Choose your preferred seat between 12 hours and 45 minutes prior to departure.

  • Simply proceed to the NOVOAIR check-in counter with your mobile check-in receipt and a valid photo ID for verification no later than 30 minutes prior to departure of the flight.
  • If you have any check-in baggage, please present your bags along with your mobile check-in receipt & a valid photo ID to our airport check-in counter 30 minutes prior to departure.
  • You will have to present the mobile boarding pass to security and to NOVOAIR Boarding Staff while boarding the aircraft.
  • Once you have checked in, you will not be able to make any changes online on our mobile app.
  • For any changes to your check-in status, please contact our airport check-in counter along with copies of your printed mobile check-in receipt 30 minutes before flight departure time. Mobile check-in is subject to cancellation if you do not report to check-in counter at least 30 minutes prior to departure. In that case, your reserved seat will be released and all existing no-show rules will apply.
  • All existing terms and conditions, cancellation & baggage rules and allowances apply.

Benefits:

  • Check-in from your home, office or on the go
  • Reserve your preferred seat
  • Skip the queue and save time at the airport
