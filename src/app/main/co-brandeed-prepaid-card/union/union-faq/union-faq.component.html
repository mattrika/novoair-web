@if (active) { } @else { } What is the annual fee of this credit card?

Annual fee for UNIONPAY PLATINUM CREDIT CARD is BDT 5000 (+ 15% VAT)= BDT 5750. Annual fee of 1st year is free with this card. From 2nd year and onwards, Annual fee will be waived if a Cardholder does at least 15 transactions before next Card Anniversary Date. This waiver will not be applicable in case of any Late Payment and Over Limit Charges during the respective period.
