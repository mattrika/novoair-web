  • SMILES Membership Tier-based Policies are :
  • SMILES Member Tiers are defined as Basic, Sapphire and Platinum based on accrued SMILES Miles (‘SMILES’) in Member Account. Tier Qualifications are Program enrolment for Basic, 600 SMILES for Sapphire, and 5,000 SMILES for Platinum Membership Tiers.
  • SMILES Members may apply for Members Status Upgrades based on Tier-based SMILES eligibility periods. Account SMILES earned within this period may be applied towards a Status Upgrade requests: 1 year for Sapphire Membership Tier, and 2 years for Platinum Membership Tier.
  • Membership status reviewed after every month end and update on 1st day of next month.
    When any member reaches SMILES Sapphire Status, an auto generated email has been send with address verification notification to his/her personal email address. Members need to verify his/her account information and confirm the mailing address. Incompletion may result; unsent SMILES Card and unable to enjoy the full benefits of SMILES program.
  • SMILES Members may apply for SMILES Rewards based on accrued SMILES Miles. SMILES are valid for a period of 2 years from the date on which a Member undertakes the relevant Flight, and will expire (and be deducted from the Member's Account) at the end of its validity period.
  • SMILES Membership Tier Status is valid for 2 years for Sapphire Membership Status, and 2 years for Platinum Membership Status.
  • Unused, expired SMILES Miles, once deducted from the Member's Account, cannot be re-credited back to the Member’s SMILES Account. It is the Member's responsibility to be aware of both the SMILES Miles in his or her Account and when the SMILES Miles will expire, using their on-line account.
  • NOVOAIR SMILES FFP reserves the right to reverse or cancel any SMILES Miles credited to a Member incorrectly, or not in accordance with, or in breach of the Program Rules at any time.
