Card Activation

Activate your card by submitting the signed Acknowledgement Slip at any EBL branch. Your card will be activated within 24 hours after submission.

Lost / Stolen Card

Call EBL 24x7 Contact Center +88028332232 or 16230 to block the Card & visit any EBL branch to apply for a replacement of Card & PIN.

Card Reload / Calendar Limit (Per Day)

  • Maximum Re-load limit : BDT 100,000
  • Payment Through POS : BDT 40,000 in 6 transactions
  • Withdrawal by ATM : BDT 50,000 in 6 transactions
