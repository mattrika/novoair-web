@if (active) { } @else { } What is EBL NOVOAIR Co-Brand Prepaid MasterCard?

With the aim to promote NOVOAIR Frequent Flyer members through SMILES program, we are offering EBL NOVOAIR Co-Branded Prepaid Mastercard. After first flight with SMILES ID, Customer can apply for NOVOAIR EBL Co-Branded Mastercard.

  • It’s a Dual currency EMV MasterCard branded prepaid card
  • Amount reloading is absolutely free
  • One can use the MasterCard prepaid card at more than 10000 shops, restaurants and ATMs worldwide
  • Discount facilities at over 1,300 partner merchants countrywide
  • Round the clock access to large ATM network (EBL ATM & All MasterCard ATMs worldwide)
@if (active) { } @else { } What is the Process Flow for
this Co-Branded Card?
  • NOVOAIR invites to their existing SMILES members to avail this Card
  • EBL or new SMILES members can enroll and request NOVOAIR to get this Card through EBL.
  • NOVOAIR will mail out the enrollment form pack to the SMILES members.
  • Once the members will revert the form to NOVOAIR, EBL will arrange to collect those completed forms & KYC (Know Your Customer) forms from NOVOAIR’s Office
  • EBL shall print the card and will post out to the SMILES members within 3 working days.
  • This Pre-Paid Card will be initializing with ZERO amount and 200 miles as SMILES points for redemption.
  • Initial card EBL Loads the amount from any EBL outlet or branch.
  • Amount can be taken out from ATM or used at POS through card.
