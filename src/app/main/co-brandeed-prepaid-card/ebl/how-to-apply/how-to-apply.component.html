How to Apply

After first flight with SMILES ID, Customer can apply for NOVOAIR EBL Co-Branded Mastercard.

Download the forms here! The forms can be digitally filled using a PDF Reader Software (Adobe Acrobat Reader preferred). Or you can print out the forms and fill-up by hand. Once filled up, please check that:

  • You have attached a Passport Size Photograph with your name written at the back
  • You have filled both the forms (Application form and KYC ) and signed with date
  • Attached your Used Ticket COPY with SMILES ID
  • You have attached required documents

After completing the forms, please courier it in below format-

NOVOAIR - EBL CO-BRANDED PREPAID CARD

House-50, Road-11, Block-F, Banani, Dhaka-1213.

Your card will be issued within a minimum of 15 working days.
