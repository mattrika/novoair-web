Photo ID is Mandatory for any Air Travel

In accordance with the circular from the Civil Aviation Authority published on 30 June 2017 (Ref: CAAB/1705/11/AT/4188), all passengers traveling by air on the domestic route must produce their valid photo identity card at the check-in counter along with the ticket. Any one of the following identity cards will be treated as a valid photo identity card:

  • Passport
  • National ID Card
  • Driving License
  • Any ID Card with Photograph
  • School/College/University ID (Students)
  • Birth Certificate / Immunization Card (Child & Infant)
